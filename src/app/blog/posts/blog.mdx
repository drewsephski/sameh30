## Technical Writing Guidelines

### Structure Your Content

1. **Executive Summary**: Brief overview of the technical problem and solution
2. **Problem Definition**: Clear description of the engineering challenge
3. **Methodology**: Detailed approach and technical analysis
4. **Results**: Quantified outcomes and performance improvements
5. **Lessons Learned**: Key insights and recommendations

### Include Technical Details

- **Data Analysis**: Charts, graphs, and statistical analysis
- **Engineering Calculations**: Mathematical models and formulas
- **Equipment Specifications**: Technical parameters and performance data
- **Economic Analysis**: Cost-benefit considerations and ROI calculations
